About Us

Your Reliable Partner in Contracting & Maintenance

COVER General Contracting and Maintenance SP LLC is a premier contracting firm based in Mussafah, Abu Dhabi. We specialize in building maintenance, roof repairs, waterproofing, and full contracting services for both industrial and residential units.

Operating across the UAE capital, our skilled team delivers quality workmanship, using premium materials and proven techniques. From a leaking roof to a complete building renovation — we cover it all.
